Homemade fish & chips

Ingredients:

  • 500g cod fillets
  • 150g plain flour
  • 150ml beer
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • Sunflower oil, for frying
  • 900g Maris Piper potatoes, peeled and cut into chips
  • Salt
  • Malt vinegar, for serving
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, beer, and egg until you have a smooth batter.
  2. Heat the oil in a deep pan or deep fat fryer to 190째C. Pat the fish fillets dry with kitchen paper and coat them lightly with a little flour. Dip the floured fish fillets into the batter. Deep-fry until golden brown and cooked through, turning occasionally.
  3. Drain on kitchen paper and sprinkle with salt. In another pan, heat the oil to 180째C. Add the chips and cook for 5 minutes. Drain on kitchen paper, then cook for a further 10 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. Drain once more on kitchen paper, then sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Serve the fish and chips with malt vinegar on the side.
