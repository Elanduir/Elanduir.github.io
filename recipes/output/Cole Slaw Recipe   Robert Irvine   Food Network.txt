                                                                    Prep:    20 min




               Cole Slaw




               6 cups shredded cabbage
               1 cup shredded carrot
               1 cup mayonnaise
               1/4 cup white wine vinegar
               1 teaspoon celery seed
               1/4 cup sugar
               Salt and pepper




               In a large bowl, combine cabbage and carrots. In a smaller bowl, make
               dressing by combining mayonnaise, vinegar, celery seed, sugar, salt and
               pepper. Toss dressing into cabbage mixture and let chill. Serve in a family
               style bowl.
