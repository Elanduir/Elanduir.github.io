           Downton Abbey Earl Grey Shortbread
           Cookies
           Crisp and buttery shortbread cookies with a hint of Earl Grey, with just
           5-ingredients, they're perfect for a Downton Abbey party, tea time, or scarfing
           down all by yourself! <---- Like I did!

           Ingredients
                 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter,
                 1/2 cup powdered sugar
                 2 cups all-purpose flour
                 1/2 tsp salt
                 1 bag Earl Grey Tea Leaves


           Instructions
              1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
              2. Cream butter and confectioners sugar in a large bowl or stand mixer.
              3. Add in flour, salt, and tea leaves. continue to mix until a soft dough forms. The mix will be dry and
                 crumbly at first but will come together.
              4. Roll dough out on a lightly floured surface until it's 1/4 inch thick.
              5. Cut out cookies using a 2 in round cookie cutter.
              6. Bake on a parchment lined baking sheet for 20-22 minutes or until edges are a golden brown.


           Notes
           For a stronger initial flavor, mix the butter and tea leaves together about 6 hours ahead of time to
           infuse.Slightly Adapted from Dine & Dish.1 cookie is 3 WW PointsPlus.
