Double Stuf OREO Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of Double Stuf OREO cookies
  • 2 cups pancake mix
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Separate the OREO cookies and set the filling aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the pancake mix, milk, mascarpone cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  4. For each pancake, pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet or griddle. Place 1 teaspoon of the OREO filling onto the center of each pancake.
  5. Cook the pancakes until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve the pancakes with whipped cream and chocolate sauce, if desired.
  7. Enjoy!
