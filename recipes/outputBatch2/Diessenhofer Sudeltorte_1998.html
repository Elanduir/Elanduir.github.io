A delicious traditional German cake.

Images

Category

D’Chuchi / Okt 1998

Ingredients

  • 500 g flour
  • 250 g butter
  • 150 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 500g apples, peeled and sliced
  • 200 g breadcrumbs
  • 200 g sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 200 g whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, butter, sugar, and egg together to make the dough.
  3. Press two thirds of the dough into a round baking pan.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix apples, breadcrumbs, sugar, and cinnamon.
  5. Spread the apple mixture over the dough in the baking pan.
  6. Roll out the remaining dough and place it on top of the apple mixture.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes.
  8. Let the cake cool and serve with whipped cream.
